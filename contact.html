FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our services and processes.

How quickly can you start on my project?

After our initial consultation and contract signing, we typically begin projects within 1-2 weeks. For urgent requirements, we offer expedited development timelines with dedicated resources.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have experience across various industries including e-commerce, fintech, healthcare, education, logistics, entertainment, and social platforms. Our team adapts quickly to industry-specific requirements.

Do you offer post-launch support?

Yes, all our projects include post-launch support. We offer various maintenance packages ranging from basic bug fixes to comprehensive 24/7 support with dedicated account managers.

Can you work with our existing development team?

Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with in-house teams as strategic partners, providing specialized expertise, additional resources, or complete project outsourcing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept wire transfers, PayPal, major credit cards, and cryptocurrency for international clients. Payment terms are flexible and can be structured per project milestones.
